# ✍️ Prompt Engineering Notes

## 📌 Overview
Prompt engineering is the process of designing inputs to get better outputs from LLMs.

---

## 🧠 Techniques
- Clear instructions
- Role-based prompting
- Step-by-step reasoning
- Few-shot examples

---

## ⚙️ Example

Bad prompt:
"Explain AI"

Good prompt:
"Explain AI in simple terms for a beginner with real-world examples"

---

## 🚀 Why it matters
- Improves response quality
- Reduces hallucinations
- Essential for LLM applications

---

## 📚 Learning Outcome
I learned how structured prompts significantly improve AI performance and reliability.
